Typically..

Typically, most days after work you can find me either walking or playing outside with my dogs, then spending the evening relaxing playing video games, painting or watching some tv. I also enjoy hanging out with my friends and doing our makeup. I really enjoy doing halloween makeup with special effects, like blood or scars.

We play very long campaigns so the stories become very special to us and it makes playing so much fun. I highly encourage anyone and everyone to step out of their comfort zone and try it!
