Dungeon Masters

Master of the Dungeon


Dungeon masters are the key to the game of Dungeons and Dragons. They are the creator and manager of the story and game. There are basic rules for the game, but the decisions are ultimately made by the dungeon master. There are many tools that dungeon masters can use to make their job easier, such as apps or organizers that help to create encounters and keep track of their story.

Every dungeon master needs a screen to hide their workspace from the rest of the party. The game is more fun when the players don't expect what is coming next.


Many dungeon masters like to use interesting and creative game tiles to create a more realistic terrain. These props can make it easier for players to imagine what the scene looks like in the dungeon masters head.
