Dungeon Masters

Polyhedral Dice


Polyhedral dice are necessary for every Dungeons and Dragons player. Aside from a playersheet or app to track your progress, all you need are your dice and imagination. You use your dice to attack enemies, determine damage dealt and for ability checks to see if you can accomplish any tasks. There are many different kinds of dice, and many D&D players accumulate large collections, while others stick with their tried and true. Personally, I am a dice hoarder and cannot be stopped from buying more and more dice.


Wyrmwood is my go-to store for all of my dice. They are beautiful, quality dice and can always be trusted to supply their customers with wonderful products.

Miniatures and Landscapes


For many D&D parties, a simple game board will suffice, but many people enjoy physical props to accompany their stories and roleplay. At the very least, it is nice to have a miniature that resembles your character. Enemy miniatures also help give your players an idea of what they are fighting and sometimes instills some much needer fear for the survival of your character.
