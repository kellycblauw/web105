Waitomo Glowworm Caves

Tourists can take a boat ride underground and see thousands of tiny glow worms illuminating the caves above them. Glow worms can be found all over Zew Zealand, but they are most often found in damp, overgrown places like the banks of lakes, rivers and forest undergrowth.

New Zealand

New Zealand is a great choice for a relaxing, scenic vacation. You will be surrounded by breathtaking scenery, like mountains, geysers, glaciers, waterfalls and even rainforests.

There are many choices for adventure or extreme activities like, sky diving, bungy jumping, jet boating, rafting, caving, zip lining, canyoning, and heli-skiing. There are also endless opportunities to experience the breathtaking nature for free by hiking, swimming in the countless natural bodies of water, and even just hanging out under the stars.
